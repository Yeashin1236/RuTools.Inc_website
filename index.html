Strategic Market Positioning

RuTools Inc. targets underserved yet rapidly growing markets in Russia, Central Asia, and the Caucasus, capitalizing on rising demand for reliable, affordable hand tools in construction, manufacturing, and DIY sectors.

Global Partnerships & Cost Advantage

The company will leverage strong sourcing relationships with top international brands (Stanley, Bosch, Daitool, Foxwoll) and manufacturers in China, India, and Bangladesh to ensure product quality and price competitiveness.

Scalable Business Model

With a phased growth strategy—starting with imports and e-commerce, transitioning to local manufacturing, and expanding through retail and export—RuTools is designed for long-term scalability and alignment with regional import substitution policies.

What we do

Here are the six main product categories offered by RuTools Inc.

Screwdrivers

Includes flathead, Phillips, Torx, and precision sets with ergonomic handles and magnetic tips.

Wrenches

Features adjustable, combination, and socket sets made from durable chrome vanadium steel.

Pliers

Includes needle-nose, slip-joint, locking, and cutting pliers with comfort grips and corrosion-resistant finishes.

Hammers

Offers claw, ball-peen, sledge, and mallet hammers with shock-absorbing handles and balanced design.

Measuring Tools

Covers tape measures, spirit levels, and calipers with high-visibility markings and magnetic bases.

Tool Kits

Comprehensive sets that combine multiple tools, packaged in durable carrying cases for both professional and DIY users.
